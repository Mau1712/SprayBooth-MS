Installation

A spray booth or spray room is a mechanically-ventilated and heated structure used to enclose or accommodate a spraying operation and curing cycle to harden paint. Spraying booths or rooms are designed to confine and limit the escape of spray, volatile vapors, mist or fuel residue, dust, odours, and exhaust. Spray booths/spray rooms and exhaust duct work shall be protected in accordance with the Building Code and operated and maintained in accordance with the Fire Code.
